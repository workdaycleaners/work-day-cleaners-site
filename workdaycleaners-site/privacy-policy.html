Privacy Policy

How we protect your information.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Work Day Cleaners collects, uses, and protects your personal information in compliance with Georgia privacy laws.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Request a quote or service
  • Contact us for information
  • Apply for employment
  • Subscribe to our communications

This may include your name, email address, phone number, address, and service preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide cleaning services and respond to your requests
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Process payments and maintain service records
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only:

  • With service providers who help us operate our business
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Regular security assessments

Georgia Privacy Rights

Under Georgia law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.
