Common questions

Frequently asked questions about Move-in Cleaning

Should I book my move-in clean before or after moving furniture in?

Before furniture arrives is ideal. It lets us clean inside every cabinet, closet, and corner without working around boxes.

What does a move-in clean include?

Full kitchen including appliance interiors, all bathrooms, inside all cabinets and closets, baseboards, window tracks, and all floors.

How long does a move-in clean take?

Usually 3 to 6 hours depending on your home size.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes. All cleaning products and equipment are included. Nothing to provide on your end.

Are you bonded and insured?

Yes. Work Day Cleaners is fully bonded and insured for all residential cleaning services.
