Common questions

Frequently asked questions about Deep Cleaning

How long does a deep clean take?

Typically 4 to 8 hours depending on your home size and current condition.

Do I need to be home during the deep clean?

No. You do not need to be present. You just need to arrange access and we handle everything.

What does a deep clean include?

Inside appliances and cabinet interiors, tile grout, baseboards, ceiling fans, window tracks, behind furniture, and surfaces standard cleaning skips.

How is a deep clean different from regular cleaning?

A deep clean covers everything a standard visit does plus all the areas that accumulate buildup over time.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes. All professional-grade cleaning products and equipment are included.
