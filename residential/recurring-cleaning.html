Common questions

Frequently asked questions about Recurring Cleaning

Can I change my cleaning frequency?

Yes. You can switch between weekly, biweekly, or monthly scheduling at any time.

Will I have the same cleaning team each visit?

We aim to assign a consistent team to your home so they learn your preferences and priorities over time.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No. Most clients provide a key or access code. We send a notification when we arrive and when we are done.

What is included in a recurring clean?

Kitchen surfaces and appliance exteriors, all bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas, and floors. Custom checklists available.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes. All cleaning products and equipment are included in your recurring service plan.
